The best time to visit Kerala is generally during the winter months, from September to March.
Kerala is famous for tourism due to its diverse natural beauty, including backwaters, beaches, and hill stations,
along with its rich cultural heritage. Referred to as "God's Own Country", Kerala offers a variety of attractions and activities, making it a popular destination for travelers.
