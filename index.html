About Kerala

A Timeless Paradise
Awaits You

Nestled along India's southwestern coast, Kerala enchants with its lush tea gardens of Munnar, the serene backwaters of Alleppey, pristine beaches of Kovalam, and wildlife sanctuaries teeming with life.

Best visited from September to March, when the monsoon's gift transforms the land into an emerald jewel — a destination that rewards the curious traveller at every turn.
